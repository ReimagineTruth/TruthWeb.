Pi Network Mainnet Progress: What’s Next?

April 09, 2025 | TruthWeb Team

Pi Network, the mobile-first cryptocurrency project, continues to make strides in its Testnet phase as of April 2025. With over 60 million Pioneers worldwide, the anticipation for the mainnet launch is palpable. Recent updates suggest significant progress in scalability and ecosystem development.

Testnet Milestones

The Pi Core Team has successfully completed several key Testnet milestones, including the implementation of a robust KYC process and the testing of smart contracts on the Pi blockchain. These developments are crucial for ensuring a secure and decentralized network upon mainnet launch.

Additionally, the team has introduced the Pi Browser, enabling developers to build decentralized applications (dApps) that integrate seamlessly with the Pi ecosystem. This move positions Pi Network as a competitor to established blockchains like Ethereum.

What’s Next for Pioneers?

The next phase includes a phased mainnet rollout, expected to begin in late 2025. Pioneers can look forward to wallet upgrades, enabling them to hold and transact Pi coins securely. The team has also hinted at partnerships with global merchants, which could boost Pi’s real-world utility.

For now, the focus remains on community engagement and education. The TruthWeb team encourages Pioneers to stay informed and participate in upcoming Testnet events to shape the future of this groundbreaking project.
